This project presents an interactive Sales Dashboard built using sales data to analyze business performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends. The dashboard helps in making data-driven decisions by visualizing key metrics.
##dataset used
-<a href="[https://github.com/ashokakumaryadav/sales-dashboar"](https://github.com/ashokakumaryadav/sales-dashboard-/blob/main/Online%20Sales%20Data.csv)>dataset view</a>
🔹 Sales Performance Analysis
Monthly analysis of units sold from January to August
Identification of sales growth trends and fluctuations
Comparison of different payment methods (Credit Card, Debit Card, PayPal)
Revenue contribution based on payment types
DASHBOARD
-<a href=https://github.com/ashokakumaryadav/sales-dashboard-/blob/main/excel%20dashboard.jpeg)>dashboard view</a>
🔹 Product & Regional Analysis
Category-wise performance of products like Beauty, Books, Clothing, Electronics, Home Appliances, and Sports
Identification of top-performing and low-performing categories
Region-wise comparison of unit price across Asia, Europe, and North America
Monthly trend of total revenue generation
